What is Password Vault?

A password vault is like your personal digital safe—protected by one strong master password—that keeps all your login credentials, credit card details, and secret notes locked away under powerful encryption. It not only stores your passwords securely but can also generate random, complex ones, save, update, or delete them with ease, and auto-fill login forms whenever you visit websites. With secure syncing across devices, your saved credentials are always available when you need them, and everything stays encrypted so that only your master password can unlock the vault.

🔐 How It Works

A password vault helps you manage your passwords effortlessly by letting you:
Add new credentials—automatically when you log in or manually via the app. It captures site name, username, and password, and stores them encrypted
Update passwords—when you change a site password, the vault offers to replace the old one, or you can edit entries yourself
Delete outdated or unused logins with a click inside the vault’s interface
Generate strong, unique passwords—choose length and character types, and the vault creates one for you to use and save.
